There are many things you will do in the case of an overdose emergency. Re-order the following steps from 1 (first) to 5 (last).

  1. Keep them sitting or lying
  2. reorder
    Contact their FroCo (if they are a first-year)
  3. reorder
    Stay with them until help arrives.
  4. reorder
    Call the YPD: 203-442-4400
  5. reorder
    Try to keep them talking and awake.
