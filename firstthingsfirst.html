Reorder

There are many things you will do in the case of an overdose emergency.
Re-order the following steps from 1 (first) to 5 (last).

  • Keep them sitting or lying down
  • Contact their FroCo (if they are a first-year)
  • Stay with them until help arrives
  • Call the YPD: 203-442-4400
  • Try to keep them talking and awake

    • Correct! All of these steps are important, but the first thing is always to call for help. Even if you are not sure, call for help!
